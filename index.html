    How It Works

    Choose Focus (25 minutes) or Break (5 minutes), then:

    • Click Start to begin the timer.
    • Click Pause to pause, or Reset to start over.
    • When a focus session ends, the timer switches to break automatically.
    • After a break, switch back to Focus and repeat.
